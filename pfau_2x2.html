Wade Pfau Framework

The RISA Profile

The Retirement Income Style Awareness (RISA) framework helps identify which retirement income strategy best fits your personality, preferences, and goals.

Optionality  ↑  Commitment
← Probability-Based Safety-First →
🪣
Probability-Based + Commitment

Time Segmentation

Strategy: Bucket Approach

Divides assets into time-based buckets — short, medium, and long-term — to match income needs across different phases of retirement.

  • Bucket 1: Cash/bonds (1–5 yrs)
  • Bucket 2: Balanced (5–15 yrs)
  • Bucket 3: Growth (15+ yrs)
📊
Probability-Based + Optionality

Systematic Withdrawals

Strategy: Total Return

Relies on a diversified investment portfolio with flexible withdrawals. Accepts market volatility in exchange for upside potential and liquidity.

  • 4% rule / dynamic spending
  • High flexibility & control
  • Exposed to sequence-of-returns risk
🏦
Safety-First + Commitment

Income Protection

Strategy: Annuity / Income Floor

Builds a guaranteed income floor using annuities, pensions, and Social Security to cover all essential expenses — regardless of market performance.

  • Eliminates longevity & sequence risk
  • Reduces flexibility & liquidity
  • Pfau's preferred safety-first approach
🛡️
Safety-First + Optionality

Risk Wrap

Strategy: Protected Portfolio

Uses annuities or insurance products wrapped around an investment portfolio to provide downside protection while maintaining some flexibility.

  • Variable annuities with GLWB riders
  • Floor with upside participation
  • Balances protection & growth
More flexibility, market dependent More certainty, insurance dependent
